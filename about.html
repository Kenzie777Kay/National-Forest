About Us!

We are Boise Idaho National Park! We are devoted to keeping our Nations forest and lands preserved for generations to come. we survive on a volunteer basis and government funding. Our national park is dedicated to the wildlife habitats. Not only are the Trees important to the animals but they are important for us as well! around 15% of worldwide emmissions are diverted naturally by trees! To learn more about how we can keep these trees going go down to our Volunteer section.

Volunteers!

Volunteers are a always in demand. We need you! Idaho National Forest uses hundreds of volunteers each year to keep our National Forest flourishing. But you're thinking what do volunteers even do? well each year we plant around 2 thousands of trees, we make sure our forest is clear of litter and debree. We keep our trails maintained and safe for our millions of visitors each year! However, volunteering might not be right for you! In that case take a scroll on down to our donation section to see what else you can do to keep our Forests alive!
